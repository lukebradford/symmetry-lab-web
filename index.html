Symmetry Lab

Create beautiful symmetrical art, one stroke at a time.

What it does

Draw a stroke. Symmetry Lab mirrors it instantly across up to 128 axes, turning simple gestures into intricate, mesmerizing patterns.

No artistic skill required — pick up the app, make a mark, and watch something extraordinary emerge. Quietly loved by kids, hobbyists, artists, jewelry designers, and anyone looking for a few minutes of meditative play since 2009.

What you can do.

  1. 01

    Up to 128-fold symmetry

    Rotational and mirrored. Start simple; turn the dial to watch a line bloom into a wheel of reflections.

  2. 02

    A deep well of brushes

    Smooth, jagged, line, dot, and a growing set of experimental brushes with their own texture and weight.

  3. 03

    Colors that move

    Dynamic palettes — rainbow, fire, ice, and light — that shift hue and temperature as the stroke travels.

  4. 04

    Snap-to-grid modes

    Tiled, woven, and geometric grids that align each mark to a lattice, for patterns that feel almost engineered.

  5. 05

    An autonomous mode

    A pattern generator that draws on its own. Set it going and watch — it's oddly hypnotic.

  6. 06

    Share anywhere

    Send to Photos, Instagram, Messages, AirDrop — straight from iOS, no accounts, no cloud.
