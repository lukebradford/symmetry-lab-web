Symmetry Lab

Create beautiful symmetrical art in seconds. Or hours.

What it does

Draw a stroke. Symmetry Lab mirrors it instantly up to 128 times, turning simple gestures into intricate, mesmerizing patterns.

No artistic skill required: pick up the app, make a mark, and watch something extraordinary emerge. Quietly loved by hobbyists, artists, kids, and anyone looking for a few minutes of easy creativity since 2009.

Features

  1. 01

    Up to 128-fold symmetry

    Rotational and mirrored. Start simple; increase symmetry to watch a line bloom into a wheel of reflections.

  2. 02

    A deep well of brushes

    Create unique textures with jagged, dot, square, and linear brushes for an endless variety of patterns.

  3. 03

    A flexible color system

    Paint on black or paper backgrounds, build custom gradients, and choose any color you can imagine — including dynamic palettes (rainbow, fire, and ice) that shift as the stroke travels.

  4. 04

    Snap-to-grid modes

    Align each stroke to a geometric grid (square, hexagonal, and more), for patterns that feel almost engineered.

  5. 05

    An autonomous mode

    A pattern generator that draws on its own. Press play and watch a hypnotic progression of symmetrical art.

  6. 06

    Share anywhere

    Send to Photos, Instagram, Messages, AirDrop — straight from iOS, no accounts, no cloud.

Free & Pro

Free to download and draw. Custom colors, black and paper backgrounds, and up to 32-fold symmetry are free with Symmetry Lab.

Symmetry Lab Pro adds gradients (including rainbow, fire, ice, and custom gradients) and up to 128-fold symmetry, along with presets and time lapse video export. $1.99 a month, $9.99 a year, or $19.99 once. No ads, no accounts, nothing stored in the cloud.
