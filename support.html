Frequently asked questions

How do I save a pattern to my Photos library?

Tap the share button and choose Save Image from the share sheet. The share sheet is also where you’ll find options to post to social apps, AirDrop, or save to Files.

Is my artwork or data shared with anyone?

No. Your patterns stay on your device unless you explicitly share them. See the privacy policy for full details.

I found a bug or have a feature request.

Please email me at lukebradfordapps@gmail.com. Screenshots, screen recordings, and steps to reproduce are enormously helpful.

Which devices and iOS versions are supported?

Symmetry Lab runs on iPhone and iPad. Check the App Store listing for the current minimum iOS version.
